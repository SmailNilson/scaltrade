1. Who we are

ScalTrade (“we”, “us”) operates the website at scaltrade.com and the ScalTrade platform. This policy explains what personal data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — email address and authentication details you provide.
  • Usage data — interactions with the platform, trades and journal entries you create, and diagnostic logs.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, and device information, collected automatically when you visit the site.
  • Broker connection data — when you connect a broker (e.g., Interactive Brokers), we process the order, position, and market data required to provide the Service. We do not store your broker password.

3. How we use data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To analyze trades and generate AI Mentor feedback.
  • To communicate with you about your account and product updates.
  • To secure the Service and prevent abuse.

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we process data on the bases of contract performance, our legitimate interests, your consent (where required), and legal obligations.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the platform (e.g., hosting, infrastructure) under appropriate confidentiality obligations, and with third parties you explicitly connect, such as your broker.

6. Cookies and analytics

The site may use essential cookies and, if enabled, privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@scaltrade.com.

9. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with an updated date.

11. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@scaltrade.com.
